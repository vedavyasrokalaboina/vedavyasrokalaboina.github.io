Business Jet Design, Analysis and Simulation

This project involved the conceptual design and aerodynamic analysis of an eight-passenger business jet aircraft. The design process began with the calculation of the aircraft's Take-Off Weight (TOW), followed by determining the key aircraft dimensions including wing span, fuselage length and aerodynamic configuration.

A complete 3D CAD model of the aircraft was developed using SolidWorks. The model was then analysed using simulation tools to evaluate aerodynamic performance, pressure distribution and velocity characteristics. The aircraft model was also tested within the Merlin flight simulator to validate the design behaviour and flight characteristics.
