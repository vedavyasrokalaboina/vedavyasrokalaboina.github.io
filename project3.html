Business Jet Design, Analysis and Simulation

Designed a conceptual eight-passenger business jet by performing a complete preliminary aircraft design process focused on estimating the aircraft’s overall weight, geometry, propulsion requirements, and flight performance characteristics. The project began with the calculation of the Take-Off Weight (TOW) based on mission requirements including passenger capacity, fuel fraction, payload, and range assumptions. Using these design constraints, the aircraft’s key parameters such as wing loading, thrust-to-weight ratio, and aerodynamic configuration were determined to ensure efficient cruise performance and stable flight behaviour. MATLAB was used to perform numerical calculations and develop scripts that determined the aircraft’s primary geometric dimensions, including wing span, wing area, fuselage length, and tail sizing. The computational model also helped estimate the required engine thrust and propulsion characteristics needed to achieve the target performance during take-off, climb, cruise, and landing phases of flight. Following the conceptual and analytical design phase, a detailed 3D CAD model of the aircraft was created using SolidWorks, representing the complete external configuration including fuselage, wing, empennage, and propulsion integration. Structural and aerodynamic feasibility of the design was further examined using SolidWorks Simulation, which allowed evaluation of structural behaviour under aerodynamic loading conditions to ensure the design maintained structural integrity while minimising weight. To evaluate the aircraft’s operational performance, the designed model was tested within the Merlin flight simulator, enabling simulation of real-world flight conditions including take-off, cruise, manoeuvring, and landing phases. The simulation results demonstrated stable flight behaviour and minimal performance deviations across these flight regimes, indicating that the conceptual design achieved acceptable aerodynamic efficiency, controllability, and performance accuracy for a preliminary business jet configuration.
