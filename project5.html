RC Plane Modelling

This project involved the design and construction of my first radio-controlled (RC) aircraft during my Bachelor's degree. The aircraft was developed as part of an introductory aeromodelling project where the objective was to design, build and successfully fly a stable RC aircraft.

The aircraft achieved its first successful continuous flight and safe landing, demonstrating stable flight performance and effective control. The project was awarded the Best Project Prize at the university for its successful design and flight demonstration.

This achievement provided me with the opportunity to join the ARDC (Aero Research and Development Club) team at the university, where I later participated in more advanced aircraft design and aeromodelling projects.
