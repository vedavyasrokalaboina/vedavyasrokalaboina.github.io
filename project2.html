SAE Aero Design Competition

Served as Technical Lead for the aircraft design team participating in both SAE International Aero Design Competition and SAE National Aero Design Competition. The project involved aerodynamic analysis, structural design, CAD modelling and performance optimisation of a radio-controlled aircraft.

The team achieved strong results including participation in the SAE Aero International Competition in Florida, USA and securing third place in the SAE Aero National Competition in Chennai, India.
