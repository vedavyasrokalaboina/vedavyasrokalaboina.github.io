Bio-Inspired Blended Wing Body Aircraft

Designed and optimised a bio-inspired Blended-Wing-Body (BWB) aircraft using SolidWorks and ANSYS Fluent. Validated the CFD framework through rigorous mesh convergence and external benchmarking. Conducted a parametric study on winglet sweep angles, identifying the configuration that maximised the lift-to-drag ratio and confirmed superior aerodynamic efficiency.
